    About

    My infatuation with design had humble beginnings. I would sketch layouts of nearly every room inside my childhood home in Philadelphia, convinced I could find a configuration that just felt better. It wasn’t uncommon for my mother to come home at the end of the day and find the living room layout completely changed. While my drawing skills have most certainly improved since then, one fact that has not changed is the enthusiasm I feel when given the opportunity to create something that is not only captivating, but safe and functional.
